Events of Gpss

Let see some events of Gpss:

Annual Sports Day

Gpss sports day Sports days (British English) or field days (American English) are events staged by many schools and offices in which people participate in competitive sporting activities, often with the aim of winning trophies or prizes. Though they are often held at the beginning of summer, they are staged in the autumn or spring seasons, especially in countries where the summer is very harsh. Schools stage many sports days in which children participate in the sporting events. It is usually held in elementary schools.

Annual Cultural Program

Gpss Cultural program Every year the school celebrates Annual Events during the month of Nov / Dec in the form of Sports or Cultural programs. These programs are organised every year alternately. The Annual Cultural Program gives an opportunity for students to present their talents in music, dance and drama. The school organises a two day Annual Cultural Program where Nursery to Class 5 students perform on the 1st Day and students of Class 6 to 12 perform on the 2nd Day. Students get an opportunity to present themselves before parents and guests and the remaining students learn about backstage work and team work and the importance of coordination. The energised and motivated students showcase colour filled and theme oriented performance.
